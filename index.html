About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Control and Dynamical Systems at California Institute of Technology, working on modeling and analysis of synthetic biological circuits. I am working with Richard Murray in the Biocircuits Lab. Refer to the Publications page for more information on my current and past work. More informal technical posts are on the Blog page.

Before joining Caltech, I graduated with a bachelors and a masters degree from the Indian Institute of Technoloy (IIT) Kharagpur, India. My Bachelor of Technology degree is in instrumentation engineering and Master of Technology in control systems engineering from the Electrical Engineering department.

Research Interests

  • Control theory
  • Systems and synthetic biology
  • Robustness of nonlinear systems
  • Artificial intelligence methods for systems modeling
